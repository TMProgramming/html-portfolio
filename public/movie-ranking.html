The Best Film Series According to Tony

Top 3 Movie Series

The Original Star Wars Trilogy

This series was the one that sparked my love of film and Sci-Fi. I will say however, I prefer the pre-specialized versions.

Friday The 13th

Although not all films in the series are as good as others, it still started my love of slasher films.

Jason Bourne

For me this is the quentessential spy thriller series. Yes, even over the 007 films as it has a much more connected and engaging storyline between the films.
